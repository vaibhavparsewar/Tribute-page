What I Admire About

Mark Zuckerberg

(Co.founder and Chief Executive Officer of Facebook)

Not caring about Money and Lifestyle : Mark Zuckerberg slept on a mattress on the floor in a small apartment years after he could have afforded much more.When Facebook started making money, had he wanted he might have started leading a luxurious life. But Mark didn't do it. Instead he just focused on taking Facebook to greater heights.

Not willing to have a boss :Yahoo offered to buy Facebook that to for 1 Billion dollars.Everyone in that company wanted Mark to say yes. Even Chris Hughes, his friend and Co-Founder of Facebook wished the same. Had Mark decided to sell his company, that would have made everyone in it, a millionaire. It was an unimaginable amount for them. Everyone would have said yes. If not, at the maximum, they would have negotiated with Yahoo to get more money.
